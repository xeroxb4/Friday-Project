DARK SHADOW

the mist-shrouded forests, strange occurrences haunt
the inhabitants every night. As the clock strikes mid-
night, eerie shadows begin to materialize, preying on
the fears and vulnerabilities of the townsfolk.
The story follows Emily, a young woman who returns
to Ravenwood after receiving a cryptic letter from her
estranged grandmother, warning her of impending danger.
